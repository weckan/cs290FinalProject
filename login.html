This site is intended to be an information hub for small-scale community volunteer groups. Natural areas are frequently the first and hardest hit by invasive species, and it is helpful to know what species of concern are present in a given spot, and what steps are necessary at a given time. For example, some plants must be pulled before seed pods are present, while others may have a specific technique necessary for reliable eradication. This site allows viewing of information on local sites and species, and provides a foundation for users to follow specific sites, add new information, and post content for other volunteers. Not present yet is message board or directory functionality, but these would be the next logical steps.
